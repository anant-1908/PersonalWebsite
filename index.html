anant1908 — The Idea Refinery

A Collection of My Essays

Don't think of this as a blog. Think of this more as an 'idea refinery'. I examine interesting ideas and take notes on them. You might find some of mine too.

If you're wondering why this site looks like a shitty internet website from the 2000s, it's because I lack CSS skills. You will find ideas here that you disagree with, agree with, or couldn't care for. My objective here is to make you think about some of these ideas yourself. Some of the writings here are going to be quite personal. If you know me, there' a good chance you'll look at me differently, or may even start disliking me. That is fine. That is necessary. There are no conformist original thinkers. Why exactly does this exist, and why do I call it an idea refinery? I want to think just as much as you do. The reason I wanted to start this blog is because I want to think. Putting this in the public domain for anyone else to read forces me to be cohesive.It also introduces my ideas to other people. However, I think I’m starting ‘this’ for myself, just as much as I’m putting it out here for a stray internet user to read. A logical question that follows is: why not journal instead? The answer: thoughts are impermanent and spontaneous. One of my friends called an unlisted video published on my YouTube: ‘a stream of consciousness. While I adore the sentiment with which she told me this, it is precisely this ‘stream’ that makes my thoughts unstructured. With a journal, I have no incentive to make any sense. There’s nothing that ties the whole thing together. Publishing here, on the other hand, forces me to make some logical sense.
